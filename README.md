## Project Overview
This project presents an HR analytics dashboard developed in Power BI,
focused on workforce distribution, salary analysis, and performance
indicators.

The objective is to provide a structured overview of employee data
to support organizational and operational decision-making.

## Dataset
The dataset includes information about:
- Departments
- Employee age distribution
- Salary levels
- Performance categories
- Total workforce metrics

## Tools
- Power BI
- Excel

## Key Metrics
- Total employees
- Average salary
- Employees by department
- Salary distribution by department
- Age distribution
- Performance evaluation categories

## Analysis & Insights
- Workforce concentration is significantly higher in specific departments.
- Salary averages vary considerably between operational and administrative roles.
- Certain age ranges represent the majority of employees, which may impact long-term workforce planning.
- Performance distribution indicates most employees fall within expected evaluation ranges.

## Conclusion
The dashboard enables HR and management teams to quickly understand
organizational structure, compensation distribution, and workforce trends.

## Notes
This dashboard was originally developed as part of a Power BI training
program and later structured and documented for professional portfolio use.
